About Us

A Space to Create

##

The OHS Art Club is a student-led creative community dedicated to the exploration and celebration of visual art in all its forms. We work to create a space for students to explore their interests in art and to create a community of support and learning.

We believe art is not a talent you either have or don't — it is something that anyone can learn. Whether you have never picked up a brush or are already selling your work online, there is a place for you here.

Every semester we run workshops, art showcases, and other events! All skill levels and artistic interests are welcome.
